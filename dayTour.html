Day Tours in Nepal

Thamel

1 day|Max :N/A

Thamel is the city's primary nightlife zone and is a bustling, visitor-friendly neighbourhood. Live music while bustling eateries serve Nepalese and foreign cuisine.


Halesi

1 day|Max :N/A

The Halesi-Maratika Caves are located next to the village of Mahadevasthan, in the Khotang District of eastern Nepal, 3,100 ft. – 4,734 ft. above sea level.


Patan

1 day|Max :N/A

Patan is the third-largest city in Nepal right after Kathmandu and Pokhara. It is presently known as Lalitpur. .


Kathmandu

1 day|Max :N/A

Kathmandu, Nepal's capital, is set in a valley surrounded by the Himalayan mountains. At the heart of the old city’s mazelike alleys is Durbar Square, which becomes frenetic during Indra Jatra, a religious festival featuring masked dances.


Boudhanath

1 day|Max :N/A

Named after its immense stupa, Boudhha is a pilgrimage destination for Tibetans. Devotees stroll around the gleaming white dome adorned with painted Buddha eyes and visit nearby monasteries and schools for prayers and seminars.


Bhaktapur

1 day|Max :N/A

Bhaktapur, known locally as Khwopa, is a city in the east corner of the Kathmandu Valley in Nepal.Bhaktapur is notable for its traditional art and architecture, historical monuments and craftwork, pottery and weaving industries, and cultural facets.
