Nepal is a landlocked country situated in southern Asia. Nepal is one of the most beautiful countries in terms of Natural resources and its culture. It is one of the most sought tourist destinations in the world because of its exceptional beauty.

So, we will discuss the amazing things about Nepal.

1. Birth of Gautam Budhha – Lumbini

Gautam Buddha also is known as “Light of Asia “- Founder of Buddhism, was born in Lumbini, Nepal. Lumbini is a sacred place as well as a place for peace of mind and relaxation. It is one of the most important Spiritual sites in the world. Mayadevi temple, Inscriptions in Ashoka pillar, Lumbini Garden are the important Significance of this place

2. Top of The World – Mount Everest

Mt. Everest is the highest mountain in the world with a height of 8848m from sea level, located in the Mahalangur Himal sub-range of the Himalayas. You are surely not going to miss the opportunity to climb Mt. Everest. The journey to the top of the world and the breathtaking view is a once in life Opportunity.

3. UNESCO World Heritage Site

UNESCO is a UN body which made a world heritage site because of their extraordinary importance and value. These places are listed in World Heritage sites by UNESCO and must visit places if you are in Nepal.

  • Kathmandu Durbar Sqaure
  • Bhaktapur Durbar Square
  • Patan Durbar Square
  • Swayambhunath
  • Pashupatinath temple
  • Boudhanath
  • Changunarayan
  • Chitwan National Park
  • Sagarmatha National Park

4. Kathmandu – The City Of Temples

Kathmandu is also the capital city of Nepal. When you first land in Nepal, it is your very first destination. The historical temples from ancient times show how our ancestors lived in the past. The craft work, the images, the design, shows how well and beautiful our culture is, carrying a lot of historical and traditional information.

5. The Country Of Himalayas

Along with Mt. Everest, there are many more Mountains. Eight of the world tallest mountains lies in Nepal. So there’s plenty of places to for Trekking or hiking. Some of the famous mountains for trekking are Annapurna base camp, Everest base camp, Manaslu Circuit Trek. You definitely should try one of these, when you are In Nepal.

6. Low Cost To Travel And Live

Nepal is one of the most affordable countries to travel to. In a small budget, you can travel famous parts of the country. The traveling cost is also low, around $10 for a meal a day and if you want to stay in a good hotel then $20 a day is enough.

7. Rich In Natural Resources

Nepal is one of the richest countries in natural resources as well as the second richest Richest country in water resources. Six thousand water resources flow through this country and the vast majority of forest cover the country, so there is enough area for you to enjoy in the lap of Nature.

8. Great Place For Hiking And Adventure

Known as the country of Himalayas, you do not want to miss the chance to go on Hiking and take an adventure. These are the famous places for hiking for tourist as well as Nepalese.

  • Nagarkot Hiking
  • Mardi Himal Trekking
  • Chisapani Hiking
  • Shivapuri Hiking
  • Namo Buddha Hiking
  • Godavari Hiking

9. Great Food

Many people are anxious about the food when they travel to different land but don’t worry- Nepali food is amazing. The famous “Dal Bhat Tarkari”, National food of Nepal “ Gundruk and Dhindo”, mouth Watering and finger leaking Newari Cuisine are some of the dishes that you must try if you really want to taste the Real Nepali Flavor.

10. Safe Country

Nepal is one of the safest places to travel to. It’s a country of Brave Gorkhas and we Nepali Believe in “Atithi Devo Bhava” meaning treating guest like a god. People are very generous and kind and if you are from an English Country, then there is only a little barrier because most of the people in a city area can communicate in English.
