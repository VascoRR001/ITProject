Nepal is a country of Himalayas, Rivers, Peaks and many more mighty natural resources. The major portion of this nation is untouched by the modern world. It is the second richest country in water resources, and one of the richest country in Natural resources. Nepal is known to the world as “Country of Himalayas”. These things make Nepal a perfect destination place.

When you are in Nepal, you will have plenty of options for Adventurous activities. The very first thing and topmost priority thing for a tourist or any Nepali is – Trekking.

Trekking is the most loved activity for any tourist. Nepal is home to the tallest mountain – Mt. Everest and eight of the tallest mountains lies in Nepal. Everest region trek, Annapurna region trek, Manaslu region trek, and Langtang region trek, are the very famous trekking region that most tourist as well as Nepalese offer.

Rafting is one of the top priority activity for an adventure lover. Rivers of Nepal are fast following originated from the Himalayas. These rivers originated by cutting the mountains or because of the landslide. Therefore, the path is mostly filled with huge rocks and high waves. Rafting in these rivers will absolutely give an adrenaline rush.

Other popular adventurous activities include Sky diving. You can skydive in front of the tallest mountains. Pokhara is quite a popular place for Sky diving. Bunjee Jump is also a popular adventurous activity that one can try. Though the excitement last for few seconds still it is one of the most loved activity. Zip flying has been quite popular in the current scenario. Nepal has the world’s longest zip line moving forward with a speed of 140km per hour. Zip flying is one of the enjoyable activity.

The other popular sport is Canyoning. This is the perfect adventure for the water sports lover. Other adventurous activities in Nepal are Paragliding, Mountain Climbing, Mountain biking and many more.
