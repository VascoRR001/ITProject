Everest base camp trek is one of the trekking destinations for trekkers that offers breathtaking views and an unforgettable menu for all trekking enthusiasts. This hiking package includes views of Mt. Everest (8848m, 29029ft), Lhotse (8516m, 27940ft), Lhotse Shar (8383m, 27503ft), (Nuptse shar 7849m), Makalu (8485m 27838ft), AmaDablam (6,812m, 22,349 ft.) (Island peak), Lhotse Shar (83 (Alt 6189m.) (Lobuche east 6119m.)Many other well-known mountain ranges are included. The Sherpa people live in this area. The most attractive monasteries in this region are found in Namche Bazaar, Tengboche, and Khumjung monastery. In the Tengboche monastery, we may see the image of evening prayer. You'll pass through various local Sherpa villages, a suspension bridge, and a waterfall on your route up to Everest base camp. This trip also includes a visit to the Sagarmatha National Park. We can also see Sherpa celebrations like Lhosar (January/February) and Dumji (June/July), which are lovely and only found at high mountain altitudes. With mask dance and family get-togethers, both of these festivities are highly interesting. We can contribute to Khumjung monastery and witness the mythical Yeti's skull in exchange for a donation. And because all of the buildings in Khumjung have green roofs, the area is known as the "green valley." This trek's main attraction is the natural and cultural beauty.
